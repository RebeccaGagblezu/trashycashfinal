ABOUT US

At Trashy Cash we broker recyclables. We act as middlemen between recycling companies and people who want to recycle. We believe that everyone can help make our country clean if they are given avenue and through our initiatives people can sit in the comfort of their homes and make the clean Ghana we all desire a reality.

We have several innovations through which we achieve our raison d' tree. Through these innovations the recyclables don't go to the dump site, they come to us then we take them straight to the recycling companies. So if you're wondering how you can also help make our great country clean don't fret, check out our various initiatives and see which one suits you or contact us for more information. Remember, a clean and beautiful Ghana begins with you and I.

PURPLE BAGS

Does the site of plastic waste on our streets and in our gutters bother you? You're not alone. It bothers us too that's why we've created a simple but genius solution to curb and soon eradicate the problem. We call it the purple bag program. With the purple bag program you get to keep plastic waste off our streets. It's super simple.

Click on the "request purple bag" . A purple bag will be brought straight to your doorstep. You will fill the bag with any plastic waste that is generated in your home, office, shop etc. When it gets full you simply schedule a pickup. You locate the "request a pickup" button on the page and then we come for the bag and give you a new one if you want to continue collecting.

Simple right? Through this project you will help keep plastic waste off our streets and make our homeland clean and beautiful all from the comfort of your home.

At Trashy Cash we believe that a "litter-free" Ghana is very achievable, but it will only be made real if we all play our part.
